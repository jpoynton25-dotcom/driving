Show Me Tell Me Questions: What to Expect and How to Answer

These questions are easy marks when you prepare properly. Use this page to build short, confident answers for test day.

Secure easy marks

  • Know likely question themes before test day
  • Answer using a clear step-by-step format

High-yield examples

Common show me tell me topics

💡

Vehicle lights

Explain how to check lights are working and when to use them.

🧼

Windscreen and washers

Show how to operate washers and keep visibility safe in all conditions.

🛞

Tyre safety checks

Know legal tread depth and what condition checks matter.

🛑

Brakes and warnings

State how to identify brake issues and warning-light concerns.

Answer method

Use this three-step response

  • Name the system clearly (for example, lights, tyres, or demister)
  • Explain the exact check or control action you would take
  • State the safety outcome you are confirming

Examiner expectation

You do not need long speeches. Keep answers clear, accurate, and practical.
