Manual vs automatic

Simple guidance to choose the right transmission

Manual lessons give broader licence flexibility and clutch control experience. Automatic lessons can feel simpler for nervous learners and often improve early confidence.

  • Choose manual if you want maximum future flexibility
  • Choose automatic if you want smoother early control and focus
  • Not sure yet? Ask for a recommendation based on confidence and timeline

Keep it practical

The best choice is the one that helps you progress consistently, not the one that sounds hardest or easiest on paper.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer manual and automatic lessons?+

Yes. We support both manual and automatic lesson recommendations.

Can I enquire if I’ve failed before?+

Yes. Many learners enquire after a fail to get a clearer route and confidence plan.

Are mock-test lessons available?+

Yes. Mock-test lessons are available for learners preparing for practical test conditions.

What if I’m nervous or returning to driving?+

We can recommend calmer-paced lesson routes for nervous or returning drivers.

Can I still use the Pass Pack without lessons?+

Yes. Pass Pack can be used independently, with lessons added only if needed.
