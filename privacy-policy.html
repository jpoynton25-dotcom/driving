Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you contact Pass Faster UK.

Quick summary

  • We collect only data you submit in forms.
  • We use data to respond to learner-support requests.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

1. Data we collect

When you submit a form, we may collect your name, email, mobile number, postcode, support preferences, and any message you provide.

2. How we use your data

We use your details to send requested resources, respond to enquiries, recommend next steps, and maintain internal lead records.

3. Storage and processing

Form data is stored in our Google Sheet lead system and email inbox for operational follow-up.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is only used for Pass Faster UK support and administration.

5. Data retention

We retain enquiry data for as long as needed to provide support, maintain records, and meet legal obligations.

6. Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting infopassfasteruk@gmail.com.

7. Contact

For any privacy questions, email infopassfasteruk@gmail.com.
