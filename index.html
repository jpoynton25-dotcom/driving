No. Pass Faster UK combines free resources, digital products, practical prep support, lesson options, and intensive course enquiries.

Can I use free resources before buying a Pass Pack?+

Yes. Many learners start with the free plan and mock support first, then upgrade when they want faster progress.

Pass Pack gives core revision and practical support. Pro adds deeper mock-test and last-week confidence support for urgent test dates.

Yes. We provide hazard confidence guidance, common fail-point support, and pre-test checklists to reduce avoidable errors.

Can I still enquire about lessons and intensive courses?+

Yes. The same form handles manual and automatic lessons, plus intensive options based on your level and timeline.
