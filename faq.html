Answers by Topic

Free resources

Free support before you buy

Are the free resources really free?+

Yes. You can use the free revision plan, mock test, and guide resources at no cost.

Is Pass Faster UK only for driving lessons?+

No. Pass Faster UK supports free resources, digital Pass Packs, practical prep guidance, lesson routes, and intensive enquiries.

Where should I start first?+

Start with the free mock theory test and then use the free resource hub to build structure.

Pass Pack

Product guidance and differences

What is included in Pass Pack?+

Pass Pack includes theory prioritisation, hazard confidence support, and practical prep resources. See full details on the Pass Pack page.

What is the difference between Starter and Pro?+

Pro includes everything in Starter plus deeper mock support and extra confidence-focused practical prep materials.

Can I use Pass Pack without booking lessons?+

Yes. Pass Pack is a standalone digital support route and can be used independently.

Theory and hazard help

Prepare both parts with confidence

Do you cover hazard perception and practical prep?+

Yes. We cover hazard perception, theory priorities, and practical prep so your support is connected rather than fragmented.

Should I revise hazard separately from theory?+

Yes. Hazard requires dedicated practice. Use the hazard support page alongside theory revision.

Is this the official DVSA site?+

No. Pass Faster UK is an independent learner-driver support platform.

Practical test support

Guidance before practical test day

Can I still enquire if I have failed before?+

Yes. Many learners enquire after a fail to get a clearer practical recovery plan.

Do you cover Show Me Tell Me help?+

Yes. Practical support includes Show Me Tell Me preparation and test-day checklists.

Where can I find practical prep support?+

Visit the Practical Test Support page for focused guidance.

Driving lessons

Lesson route and fit

Do you offer manual and automatic options?+

Yes. We support manual and automatic recommendations based on your stage and goals.

Are mock-test lessons available?+

Yes. Mock-test lesson routes are available for learners close to practical test day.

How do I request lesson support?+

Use the lesson recommendation form.

Intensive courses

Fast-track options with realistic planning

Are intensive courses suitable for beginners?+

Sometimes. Suitability depends on confidence, availability, and realistic timeline planning.

Do I need to have passed theory first?+

Passing theory usually makes intensive planning and practical timelines more effective.

Where can I request intensive options?+

Use the intensive enquiry form.

Contact and response times

Support process and next steps

How quickly do you respond?+

We aim to respond to enquiries within 1 working day.

Can I ask about more than one thing?+

Yes. Include all needs in one enquiry and we will recommend your best overall route.

Can I use this before booking anything?+

Yes. Use Get My Plan for a no-obligation recommendation first.
